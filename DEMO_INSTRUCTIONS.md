# Demo Instructions

## How to Test the App

### 1. Authentication
Since the backend might not be running, the app uses local storage for authentication.

**Test Login Credentials:**
- Email: `test@example.com`
- Password: `password123`

**Or Register a New Account:**
- Fill out the signup form with any valid details
- The account will be stored locally

### 2. Browse Restaurants
- The homepage shows 6 mock restaurants
- Click on any restaurant card to view their menu

### 3. Add Items to Cart
- Go to any restaurant menu
- Click "Add to Cart" on any food item
- You'll see a floating cart button at the bottom

### 4. View Cart & Checkout
- Click the floating cart button or cart icon in navbar
- Proceed through checkout process
- Complete order placement

### 5. Order Tracking
- After placing an order, you'll be redirected to order tracking
- Watch the animated progress indicators
